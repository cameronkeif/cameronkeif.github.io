Photograph by Hannah Koehler

Welcome to my website! My name is Cameron Keif. I am a professional software developer with five years of experience currently living in Austin, TX. I am originally from the Detroit, MI area and graduated from Michigan State University in 2014 with a B.S. in Computer Science.

I currently work at Ping Identity doing full stack web development on an authentication platform. I am always involved in our team's UI work, but I also contribute a lot to the user management and authentication/OAuth APIs. For more about what we do at Ping, check out this interview I was involved in with Built In Austin

I'm experienced with many frameworks and programming languages. In my current day to day, I work a lot with React/Redux on the frontend, as well as Java/Spring and Cassandra on the backend. I also have prior experience with PHP, C#, MySQL, and a variety of other technologies. All that said, I always strive to learn more and experiment with new languages and frameworks. Have a look at my projects page and my Github to see what I've been up to.

Lately in my career I've been focused a bit more on the front end side of things, but I definitely have the knowledge and skills to do full stack and back end development. I love making applications pretty, but I also love writing blazingly fast algorithms and database queries.
